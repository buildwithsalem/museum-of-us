My dearest Jojo,

I made this whole place for you. Every frame, every floating letter, every dim light waiting to be switched on, all of it was me trying to find a way to say the things I sometimes forget to say out loud.

You are the best thing that has ever happened to me. I never imagined living my life like this, with a man, my man, jojo man. It is amazing how much light you bring into my life, how much joy I experience around you.

2 months in, and I already felt at peace, and now, one year in, I feel at home. You are my home. You’ve changed me in ways I can’t even begin to recount, most especially in character. Made me go from hard girl, hard girl to girl who just wants her jojo 24/7.

I’m so excited to be in the time and this moment in life with you, and I can’t wait to spend forever with you, where there’s no distance and no limits. I love you more than my words could say, and I hope to show you in every way I possibly can.

With how much you’ve helped and inspired me, I pray that the same God who brought this far and didn’t kick you out of my life after relentless prayers, will strengthen and guide us as you move towards guiding us in this beautiful blessing of a relationship.

I love you, I love you, I love you more 😏

ps. Everytime I’ve squeezed your hands 3 times, I was saying, “I love you.”

— Song of Solomon 3:4

Forever yours,
Your Love
