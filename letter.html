My dearest Jojo,

I made this whole place for you. Every frame, every floating letter, every dim light waiting to be switched on — all of it was me trying to find a way to say the things I sometimes forget to say out loud.

A year ago you said "I like you" and I didn't know then that those three little words would be the beginning of something that would change the way I see everything. The way I see mornings. The way I see quiet nights. The way I see myself.

You are my favorite person to do absolutely nothing with. You are the reason my phone is too full of photos and my heart is somehow even fuller. You make loving you feel like the easiest, most natural thing I've ever done.

This flower is for you. Real ones wilt, but this one will be here whenever you come back. Consider it a little piece of something that doesn't expire — like the way I feel about you.

Thank you for being mine. Thank you for letting me be yours.

Forever yours,
Salem 🌹
